Overview

These templates are unofficial examples of how to set up and document reproducibility in Restricted Access Data Environments (RADE)

In the RADE

We will enable greater transparency of research undertaken by academics, graduate students, and agency staff. The methods and procedures developed will be applicable to many restricted access data environments. We will train researchers how to conduct research in a replicable manner, filling a void in the available curricula for replicable research. In addition, we develop new formally private verification measures adapted for replication studies.

For publication

When documenting the research that has been conducted in a RADE, properly referencing all the permissions, project numbers, and release of results is becoming increasingly important for full documentation of provenance.

These templates are examples that agencies could provide to researchers, or that researchers can request from agencies.
